Melany Olivares
UX/UI Product Designer · Remote

Hi, I'm Mel.

Product Designer with 4+ years building digital products at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and technical constraints — across payment platforms, e-commerce, and omnichannel systems.

Specialized in complex systems and scalable transactional experiences. I collaborate with engineering, product, and cross-functional teams — from discovery through delivery — using research, design systems, and data-informed decision-making.

Before product design, I spent a decade in public policy and consulting, leading multi-sector projects. That's where my ease with negotiation, facilitation, and stakeholder alignment comes from — something no design process teaches on its own.

01
I think in systems before I think in screens
Tokens, components, variants. A design that can't maintain itself isn't finished.
02
Performance is also UX
Load times, availability, and reliability are part of the experience. A slow system is a design problem.
03
I actually use AI, I don't just sell it
Claude, Figma MCP, Copilot. Tools that speed up the loop from idea to working prototype.
04
Decisions validated with research and data
Interviews, PostHog, Sentry, Grafana. Intuition is not a design plan.
